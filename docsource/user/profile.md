# Profile Settings

The profile settings can be accessed by clicking the user icon in the top right corner of the application.

## Profile Icon
You can upload a custom profile icon to personalize your account within Blazam.

## Themes
Blazam offers several color themes to customize the look and feel of the application.
* Blue
* Red
* Green
* Orange
* Gray

## Light/Dark Mode
You can toggle between light and dark modes to suit your preference or lighting conditions.
